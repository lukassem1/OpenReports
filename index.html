Beautiful report templates, ready for AI.

A free, open library of static HTML reports for your managerial meetings. Pick a design you love, download the file, and tell your AI: “make my report in this exact design.”

1

Browse & pick

Find a report design that fits your meeting — sales, finance, KPIs, marketing.

2

Download or copy the prompt

Grab the self-contained HTML file and the ready-made prompt with one click.

3

Drop it in your AI chat

Attach the file, paste your data, and get your own polished report back.
