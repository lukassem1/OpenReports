Retro: What Worked, What Didn't, Next Time

Team learnings

What worked

  • [Email delivered the lowest CAC at [$7] — far below the [$14] blended average.]
  • [Reach beat target by [20%]; top-of-funnel audiences were well-sized.]
  • [Mid-flight creative refresh lifted display CTR by [~18%].]
  • [Paid search scaled efficiently when budget was added.]

What didn't

  • [Conversions landed at [84%] of goal — the offer page under-converted.]
  • [ROAS finished at [3.2x] vs a [4.0x] target.]
  • [Display had the highest CAC at [$24] with the weakest lead quality.]
  • [Late budget reallocation cost ~[2 weeks] of efficiency.]

Next time

  • [Front-load budget into email and paid search; cap display at [10%].]
  • [A/B test the landing page before launch to protect conversion rate.]
  • [Set weekly CAC checkpoints to reallocate spend faster.]
  • [Define a stricter lead-quality threshold for display.]
